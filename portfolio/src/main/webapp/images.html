“On Canvas”


I become a part of everything I create, and
Everything I create becomes a part of me.

When I cannot identify myself through words
I always have to put my ideas down on canvas.
Anyone can speak words that will be forgotten but
I cannot forget how I feel when seeing my paintings.

When I do use words, their core pours out over my pieces
where the deepest of meanings can tell a story from only one word.

A title,
as important as the shade of color I create,
type of brush I use,
and rhythm I feel when I paint,
an identifier as important as choosing to follow my passion

I wrote this poem to express my love for painting as art form. More importantly, I explain why I give a title to every painting I create.
